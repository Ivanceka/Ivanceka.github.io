I love parrots especially Parakeets.

A parakeet is any one of many small to medium-sized species of parrot, in multiple genera, that generally have long tail feathers.
In American English, the word parakeet usually refers to the budgerigar. The budgerigar is a species of parakeet. They are notoriously good talkers. But their voices are very soft and they tend to talk rapidly so you might not recognize the fact that your budgie does indeed have a wonderful vocabulary.
